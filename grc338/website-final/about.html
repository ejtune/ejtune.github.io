Born from a shared passion for mycology and a desire to bring the bounty of the Central Coast's wild landscapes to the culinary forefront, "Forage, Fungi, Feast" is a unique foraging service founded by a dynamic group of college friends. These friends, alumni of California Polytechnic University in San Luis Obispo and former members of the esteemed Cal Poly Mycology Club, transformed their love for mushrooms into a thriving venture. United by a fascination with the diverse fungal world, they embarked on a journey to uncover the hidden treasures within the coastal woodlands.

Forage, Fungi, Feast has become a cornerstone of the Central Coast's culinary scene, connecting the flavors of the wild with the artistry of local chefs. The college friends, armed with their academic grounding and hands-on experience, traverse the region's diverse ecosystems, from Big Sur to Morro Bay, in search of the choicest mushrooms. Their commitment to sustainable foraging practices and in-depth knowledge of local fungi ensures a seasonal and diverse offering that caters to the discerning tastes of renowned restaurants and chefs.

The story of Forage, Fungi, Feast is not just about mushrooms; it's a celebration of friendship, education, and the natural abundance of California's Central Coast. Through their business, these college friends turned entrepreneurs have created a bridge between the academic world of mycology and the vibrant culinary community, fostering a connection between nature and plate that enriches the region's gastronomic landscape.
